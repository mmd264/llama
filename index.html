REAL LLAMA FACTS

Hover over images to learn about llamas, when you feel ready go to the Trivia page to see how much you know!

  • Llamas are related to the camel

    They are from South America

  • Herbivores

    Llamas survive on a plant based diet

  • Pack animals

    Llamas hold up to 75 lbs

  • Favorite food

    Llamas love to graze on grass

  • Leather

    Llama hides make great leather

  • Wool

    Their wool is used to make ropes, rugs, and fabrics

  • Ccara Llama

    Type of llama with short hair

  • Curaca Llama

    Type of llama with long hair

  • Llamas' ears

    Llamas' ears are shaped like bananas

  • Llamas are social

    They live in groups of up to 20 animals

  • Llamas defense

    Llamas are known to spit when threatened or provoked

  • Pregnancy

    Lasts 11 1/2 months and ends with a single baby
